About Devi Sri Prasad

If there's one composer who has defined the sound of 21st-century Telugu film music, Devi Sri Prasad makes a strong case. The National Award-winning musician made his soundtrack debut with songs for 1999 film Devi at the tender age of 19. Two years later, he had his first major hit with the soundtrack for Anandam, earning praise for its fusion of Telugu music with pop, rock and Western classical influences-and kicking off over two decades of dominance. Prasad is best known for racy, high-energy dance songs like 2004's "Aa Ante Amalapuram" and 2018's "Jigelu Rani", but he also excels at slow-burn romance (Nuvvostanante Nenoddantana) and folk-fusion ("Bommani Geesthe").
